About

I am studying Artificial Intelligence and Robotics for my MSc. I love the intersection between robotics and embodied intelligence through AI. My hopes for the future is one where robots and humans can cooperate towards a cleaner, more sustainable world [ What do I mean ]. In the meantime, I work and study how to enable robots to actively interact with the environment and reason about its state and their actions within said states.
